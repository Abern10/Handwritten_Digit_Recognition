How It Works

This application uses an artificial intelligence model to recognize handwritten digits. Simply upload an image containing a handwritten digit, and the model will predict the digit it represents.

The AI model is trained using a convolutional neural network (CNN) on the MNIST dataset, which consists of thousands of handwritten digits labeled with their corresponding numbers.

Feel free to try it out by uploading your own handwritten digit image!
